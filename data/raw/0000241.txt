	  <p class="ggnoads" align="center"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-442300" src="http://cdn.screenrant.com/wp-content/uploads/Sinister-Six-Movie-Spider-Man-cameo.jpg" alt="Sinister Six Movie Spider Man cameo Sinister Six Gets 2016 Release Date; Amazing Spider Man 3 in 2018" width="620" height="350" title="Sinister Six Gets 2016 Release Date; Amazing Spider Man 3 in 2018" /></p>
<p>This past week, reports began to circulate that theÂ <em>Amazing Spider-Man</em> spinoff <a href="http://screenrant.com/tag/sinister-six/" ><strong><em>Sinister Six</em></strong></a> &#8211; a film aboutÂ a team of Spider-Man super villains -Â might be in serious danger of being cancelled. Not so, it turns out, as Sony/Columbia Pictures has announced an official Fall 2016 release date for writer/director Drew Goddard&#8217;s <em>Sinister Six</em> movie. Meanwhile, <em>Amazing Spider-Man 1 &amp; 2</em> director Marc Webb&#8217;s<a href="http://screenrant.com/tag/the-amazing-spider-man-3/" ><strong><em> Amazing Spider-Man 3</em></strong></a> has been pushed to 2018 (presumably, the May 2018 spot that Sony is currently holding onto).</p>

<p>Goddard stepped down as the showrunner for Marvel Studios&#8217; upcomingÂ <a href="http://screenrant.com/tag/daredevil/" ><em>Daredevil</em> </a>series a couple months back, in order to concentrate his efforts on getting <em>Sinister Six</em> ready. However, <a href="http://screenrant.com/amazing-spider-man-3-delayed-sinister-six-venom/" >recent comments</a> made by certain other prominent members of the <em>Amazing Spider-Man</em> franchise <em>&#8220;brain trust&#8221;</em> &#8211; Roberto Orci and Alex Kurtzman, to be exact &#8211; indicated that Sony&#8217;s original plans for the series&#8217; future may&#8217;ve changed (worst case scenario, that the blueprints for buildingÂ <a href="http://screenrant.com/venom-movie-amazing-spider-man-sinister-six-sony/" >a Shared Spider-Man movie universe</a> may&#8217;ve been abandoned), in reaction to <em>Amazing Spider-Man 2</em>&#8216;s overall shaky critical reception and box office drop-off from its predecessor.</p>
<p>Sony, however, has now revealed its hand of cards, not so coincidentally as the <a href="http://screenrant.com/comic-con/" >2014 International Comic-Con</a> gets up and running. <em>Sinister Six</em> now has a November 11th, 2016 release date &#8211; one week before WB releases its Harry Potter spinoff, <a href="http://screenrant.com/tag/fantastic-beasts-and-where-to-find-them/" ><em>Fantastic Beasts &amp; Where to Find Them</em></a>. The month of November has long served franchises such as Harry Potter and James Bond well (among others), while <em>Thor: The Dark World</em> proved last year that superhero movies can also thrive at the box office during that frame.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-400179" src="http://cdn.screenrant.com/wp-content/uploads/amazing-spider-man-2-andrew-garfield.jpg" alt="amazing spider man 2 andrew garfield Sinister Six Gets 2016 Release Date; Amazing Spider Man 3 in 2018" width="620" height="320" title="Sinister Six Gets 2016 Release Date; Amazing Spider Man 3 in 2018" /></p>
<p>However, there are some pressing questions to ask about <em>Sinister Six</em>. For one, will the movie feature Andrew Garfield as Peter Parker/Spidey, seeing how the actor previously said he&#8217;s only <a href="http://screenrant.com/andrew-garfield-amazing-spider-man-4-venom-movie/" >contracted for three Spider-Man films</a>Â - and if the final movie that&#8217;s part of Garfield&#8217;s current deal is meant to beÂ <em>Amazing Spider-Man 3</em> (as would make the most sense), does that mean <em>Sinister Six</em> won&#8217;t actually featureÂ his Spider-Man?</p>
<p>Likewise, what big name actors are gong to be recruited to fill <a href="http://screenrant.com/sinister-six-movie-roster/" >the Sinister Six roster</a> that already includes Rhino (Paul Giamatti) and the Harry Osborn Green Goblin (Dane DeHaan) &#8211; not to mention, potentially Felicity Jones as Felicia Hardy/Black Cat, while Jamie Foxx as Electro remains a (slight) possibility? And how will theÂ turnout for a <em>Wild Bunch</em>-style movie with Spider-Man villains compare to other Spider-Man films, especiallyÂ given that the moviegoing public has shownÂ signs of Spidey fatigue of late? (Not to mention, there&#8217;s still thatÂ <a href="http://screenrant.com/tag/venom/" ><em>Venom</em></a> movie, waiting in the wings&#8230;)Â The <a href="http://screenrant.com/superhero-shared-movie-universe-problems/" >sustainability of a Spider-Man Shared Universe</a> is questionable, to say the least.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-396360" src="http://cdn.screenrant.com/wp-content/uploads/Sinister-Six-Amazing-Spider-Man-2.jpg" alt="Sinister Six Amazing Spider Man 2 Sinister Six Gets 2016 Release Date; Amazing Spider Man 3 in 2018" width="620" height="320" title="Sinister Six Gets 2016 Release Date; Amazing Spider Man 3 in 2018" /></p>
<p>Having said all that, the good news is that <a href="http://screenrant.com/amazing-spider-man-3-release-date-delay-sinister-six/" >releasing <em>Sinister Six</em> before <em>Amazing Spider-Man 3</em></a> frees up the latter from having to devote too much of its running time to world-building (a la <em>TheÂ Amazing Spider-Man 2</em>). Plus, Marvel Studios <a href="http://screenrant.com/marvel-studios-release-dates-2017-2018-2019/" >left the May 2018 frame</a>Â open for Sony&#8217;s next <em>Amazing Spider-Man</em> installment &#8211; which means that Webb&#8217;s film will have more time to develop, fewer larger franchise duties to fulfill, <em>and</em> less direct competition to face at the box office.</p>
<p><strong><em>Sinister Six</em></strong> opens in U.S. theaters on November 11th, 2016. <strong><em>The Amazing Spider-Man 3</em></strong> is arriving in 2018, presumably on the May 4th, 2018 date once reserved for <a href="http://screenrant.com/tag/the-amazing-spider-man-4/" ><em>Amazing Spider-Man 4</em></a>.</p>
