    <h2 class="field field-name-field-sub-headline field-type-text field-label-hidden">
      &quot;Rebel Heart&quot; and &quot;Wash All Over Me&quot; emerged in full on Nov. 28.  </h2>
<div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>When it rains, it pours.</p>
<p>On Nov. 27, a <a href="Clip%20of%20New%20Madonna%20Song%20'Rebel%20Heart'%20Leaks">snippet</a> of a new <a href="http://www.billboard.com/artist/308786/madonna">Madonna</a> song called "Rebel Heart" leaked to the Internet. A day later, a full-length version of the tune had made its way to the Web, as well as another full track, seemingly titled "Wash All Over Me."</p>
<div class="related_articles_boxout">
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="/articles/news/6334591/new-madonna-song-rebel-heart-leaks">Clip of New Madonna Song 'Rebel Heart' Leaks</a></p>
</div>
<p>"Rebel Heart" is likely a co-production with superstar DJ/producer <a href="http://www.billboard.com/artist/280336/avicii/chart">Avicii</a>, who Madonna worked with earlier in the year on new music. (Madonna posted an <a href="http://instagram.com/p/leTR0iGEVL/">image</a> of her with Avicii in a recording studio, using the hash tag #RebelHeart.) Both songs could be unfinished, or demo versions. </p>
<p>Madonna's publicist had no comment about the initial leak, though the diva's manager, Guy Oseary, <a href="https://twitter.com/guyoseary/status/538238568330584064">Tweeted</a>: "I would be grateful to any Madonna fans that can assist us in finding those responsible for the leak. We appreciate your help."</p>
<div class="related_articles_boxout">
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="/photos/6251628/madonna-album-20-possible-collaborators-instagram-2014">Who's Working on Madonna's Album? 22 Possible Collaborators</a></p>
</div>
<p>Madonna has been working on new music throughout 2014, often <a href="http://www.billboard.com/photos/6251628/madonna-album-20-possible-collaborators-instagram-2014">updating her Instagram</a> feed with photos and messages regarding her progress. A new album from the entertainer is <a href="http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop-shop/6251384/madonna-new-album-2015-diplo">expected in 2015</a>. It will be her first studio effort since 2012's <em>MDNA</em>, which debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 chart.</p>
