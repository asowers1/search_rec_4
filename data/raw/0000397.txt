			<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">List of songs recorded by The Bee Gees</h1>
						<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
									<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
								<div id="contentSub"></div>

				</div>
				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><p>The following is a table of <b>all songs recorded by <a href="/wiki/The_Bee_Gees" title="The Bee Gees" class="mw-redirect">The Bee Gees</a></b> between 1967 and 2001. Songs recorded in Australia and covers of Beatles songs are not included.</p>
<ul>
<li>The columns <b>Title</b>, <b>Year</b>, and <b>Album</b> list each song title, the year in which the song was released (unless noted - then the year the track was recorded is listed), and the official studio album or compilation album on which the song first appeared.</li>
<li>The column <b>Songwriter(s)</b> lists the writer(s) of each song.</li>
</ul>
