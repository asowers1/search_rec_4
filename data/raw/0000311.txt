This section catalogs the lyrics to every song recorded by Elton John throughout
his entire career.  It contains only songs that he recorded in studio, either as
a solo vocalist, or as a duet with other artists.  Elton has also performed several
additional songs live in concert, but those are too numerous to include here. 
Most of these songs were record for his studio albums, but there are also those
that were singles, b-sides, or tracks included on various special projects. These
lyrics are <b>unofficial</b>. Many have been transcribed by ear, and some still have
empty spaces where the vocals cannot be deciphered.<p />
Use the alphabetical links above to browse by song title, or use the lyrics search box
to find lyrics with specific words or phrases.
