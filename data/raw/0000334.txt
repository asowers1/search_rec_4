This is a project called <strong>Queen Songs </strong>comprising the information previously published (for almost 10 years) at <a href="http://queen.musichall.cz/index_en.php">Royal Legend</a>. <br>

<p><br>
<strong>What Queen Songs site offers</strong>:<br>
<br>
<strong>Tabs &amp; Chords</strong>: the biggest archive of Queen guitar tabs and chords, bassguitar, voice and drum tabs as well as fingerstyle guitar tabs<br>
<strong>Piano Sheets</strong>: the piano arrangements done by Federico Faccio.<br>
<strong>Album Analysis</strong>: analysis on the albums by Sebastian.<br>
<strong>Song Analysis</strong>: analysis on the musical features of Queen songs by Denes.<br>
<strong>Studio Info Page</strong>: first-ever made research of Queen songs by Philipp Nothaft.</p>
