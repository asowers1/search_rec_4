				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><p>The following is a <b>sortable table of all songs by <a href="/wiki/Pink_Floyd" title="Pink Floyd">Pink Floyd</a></b>:<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li>The column <b>Song</b> list the song title.</li>
<li>The column <b>Writer(s)</b> lists which member of Pink Floyd wrote the song.</li>
<li>The column <b>Lead vocal(s)</b> lists who sang lead vocals or 'N/A' in case of instrumentals.</li>
<li>The column <b>Album</b> lists the album the song is featured on.</li>
<li>The column <b>Year</b> lists the year in which the song was released.</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>
<dl>
<dd>If the song was never officially released, <b>Year</b> will list the year it was recorded.</dd>
</dl>
</dd>
</dl>
<ul>
<li>The column <b>Length</b> list the length/duration of the song.</li>
</ul>
<p>There are currently 192 songs on this list. <sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style/Dates_and_numbers#Precise_language" title="Wikipedia:Manual of Style/Dates and numbers"><span title="The text near this tag is dated.">dated info</span></a></i>]</sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes_on_non-album_songs">Notes on non-album songs</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_songs_recorded_by_Pink_Floyd&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Notes on non-album songs">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Some songs in the list were recorded but never released on any official original studio album but they were released on compilation albums or as singles. The album in the list is the first album it appeared on. These songs are:</p>
<ul>
<li>"Arnold Layne": Recorded in January 1967 and originally released as a <a href="/wiki/Single_(music)" title="Single (music)">single</a> 11 March 1967.</li>
<li>"Candy and a Currant Bun": Recorded in January 1967 and originally released as B-side to the "Arnold Layne" single 11 March 1967.</li>
<li>"See Emily Play": Recorded in mid 1967 and originally released as a single 16 June 1967.</li>
<li>"Apples and Oranges": Recorded in late 1967 and originally released as a single 18 November 1967.</li>
<li>"Paint Box": Recorded in late 1967 and originally released as B-side to the "Apples and Oranges" single 18 November 1967.</li>
<li>"It Would Be So Nice": Recorded in early 1968 and originally released as a single 13 April 1968.</li>
<li>"Julia Dream": Recorded in early 1968 and originally released as B-side to the "It Would Be So Nice" single 13 April 1968.</li>
<li>"Point Me at the Sky": Recorded in late 1968 and originally released as a single 17 December 1968.</li>
<li>"Careful with That Axe, Eugene": Recorded in November 1968 and originally released as B-side to the "Point Me at the Sky" single 17 December 1968.</li>
<li>"Biding My Time": Recorded in 1969 and originally released on the compilation album <i><a href="/wiki/Relics_(Pink_Floyd_album)" title="Relics (Pink Floyd album)" class="mw-redirect">Relics</a></i>.
<dl>
<dd>Was also a part of the show <i><a href="/wiki/The_Man_and_The_Journey" title="The Man and The Journey">The Man and The Journey</a></i> performed in 1969, then titled "Afternoon".</dd>
</dl>
</li>
</ul>
